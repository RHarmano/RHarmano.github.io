A Bit About Me

I attended Queen's University for a my Bachelor's of Applied Science in engineering physics with a mechanical option. There I had the opportunity to create unique work as part of course work and design teams. Most recently, I was able to produce a tool to generate stars along a line-of-sight from Earth to fixed point in space. I was also fortunate enough to co-captain the Queen's Fuel Cell Team, performing as electrical/mechatronic team manager while creating a manned electrical vehicle powered by a hydrogen fuel cell.

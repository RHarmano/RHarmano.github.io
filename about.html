A Bit About Me

I attend Queen's University as an Engineering Physics student in the mechanical option.

For the past few years I have been working on to create mechatronic and robotic systems, starting with a rover on the Queen's Space Engineering team and moving on to a hydrogen vehicle on the Queen's Fuel Cell Team where I spent a year as an electrical manager. During this time, I worked for a summer at IMAX to devlop software for the alignment of components in an optical relay and perform validation on existing manufacturing systems.

My time on both teams has granted me oportunities to learn about a wide variety of practical engineering skills such as PCB and CAD design, software engineering, computational physics and data science.
